Introduction

For the Wellesley Data Collective Project, we decided to scrape the Wellesley College Honors Theses Digital Archive. In doing so, we created two Jupyter Notebook tutorials, which can be found below, showing our scraping and exploration processes. Feel free to download and use our notebooks tutorials and to explore our page to view our results!

Scraping

    For the first part of our project, we scraped the Wellesley College Honors Theses Digital Archive. for Honors Theses titles, years, and departments using Selenium, BeautifulSoup, and Pandas. We stored these data in a Pandas dataframe and exported it to a json file to use in our exploration. We also hope this code can be reused in the future to scrape the most recent Senior Honors Theses.
